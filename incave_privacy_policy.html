Privacy Policy

Incave — Spatial Audio AR

Last updated: April 2026

Short version: Incave does not collect personal information, does not share your data with third parties, and all your audio files stay on your device.

📷 Camera & Microphone

Incave uses your device camera exclusively to power ARKit — mapping your environment and tracking your position in real time. The microphone is accessed by the audio engine required to play spatial sound through your device speakers.

Neither camera nor microphone data is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted. All processing happens on-device in real time.

🎵 Audio Files You Import

When you import audio files into Incave (via the Files app, share sheet, or in-app picker), they are saved to your device's local storage only. They are never uploaded to any server or shared with anyone.

You can delete imported files at any time by swiping left on them in the "Your Sounds" list.

📍 Zone Data

Spatial audio zones you create (position, radius, assigned sound) are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage. This data is not synced to any cloud service and is deleted when you reset the app or uninstall it.

📊 Anonymous Analytics

Incave uses TelemetryDeck to collect basic, anonymous usage signals — for example, that a zone was created or a sound was imported. This helps understand how the app is used and improve it over time.

TelemetryDeck is designed to be privacy-first:

  • No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Signals are hashed and cannot be traced back to you.
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA) are used.
  • Data is stored on servers in the European Union.
  • TelemetryDeck is GDPR and CCPA compliant.

You can read TelemetryDeck's own privacy policy at telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

🤝 Third-Party Sharing

Incave does not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for advertising or commercial purposes. The only third-party service used is TelemetryDeck for anonymous analytics, described above.

👶 Children's Privacy

Incave does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not contain any features directed at children and does not collect personal data from any user.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in a material way, the updated version will be posted here with a new date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
