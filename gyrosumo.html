Legal Documentation for GyroSumo

1. PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

GyroSumo ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection & Processing

GyroSumo is a "Local-Only" application. We do not use any central servers to store your personal data.

  • Motion Data: The App uses your device’s Accelerometer and Gyroscope (via CoreMotion) to detect physical impacts and movement during the "Direct Sync" and "Spatial Negotiation" protocols.
  • Camera & AR Data: The App uses the camera and ARKit/RealityKit for spatial tracking. This data is used strictly to calculate your relative position in the digital "Spatial Grid" and is processed in real-time on your device.
  • Network Usage: The App uses local network discovery (Bonjour) to allow peer-to-peer synchronization with other devices in your immediate physical vicinity. No data is transmitted over the internet to the developer or third parties.
  • Analytics: The App utilizes TelemetryDeck for anonymous usage analytics (e.g., protocol selection, feature usage). No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected or stored.

None of the raw sensor or camera data is stored or transmitted. All spatial calculations and motion detections happen locally and are discarded after the session ends.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any personal data with third parties. Anonymous telemetry is used solely to improve the technical performance of the application.
