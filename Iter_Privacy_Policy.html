🛡️ Iter: Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

"O camiño non se percorre, vívese." (The path is not traveled, it is lived.)

At Iter, we believe your journey is your own. It's a tool that remembers your path so you don't have to, without ever compromising your privacy.

2. Data Storage & Sovereignty

Currently, Iter stores all data exclusively on your device.

  • Local Sovereignty: All location data captured by Iter is stored in encrypted local storage (SwiftData/SQLite).
  • No Automatic Cloud Uploads: Iter does not automatically transmit your location history to any external servers or third-party cloud providers.
  • Future Sync Features: If Iter introduces multi-device synchronization or cloud backup in the future, these features will be strictly opt-in. You will always maintain full control over your data.

3. Location Data Usage

Iter uses your device's location services (GPS, WiFi, Cellular) to:

  1. Passive Memory: Automatically log your path in the background.
  2. Manual Anchors: Save specific coordinates at your request (e.g., parking, cafes, mementos).

This data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app on your device.

4. Analytics & Third-Party Services

Iter is a tool for you, not for advertisers.

By default, the developer has zero access to your location history. This data remains on your device unless you explicitly opt-in to future cloud-syncing or backup features as described in Section 2. Iter does not include advertising SDKs or analytics that profile your behavior. We do not share your location data with any third parties.

To help us improve the app, we use TelemetryDeck for anonymous usage analytics. TelemetryDeck helps us understand how the app is being used (e.g., which features are popular) without collecting any personally identifiable information (PII) or location data. Your usage data is anonymized and encrypted before it is sent to TelemetryDeck.

5. Data Control & Export

  • Exporting: You have full control over your data. If you choose to export your location history (e.g., as a GPX or JSON file), that file is handled via the standard iOS share sheet. We do not track or store your exports.
  • Deletion: Deleting the Iter app from your device will permanently remove all location data associated with the app. This action cannot be undone, as we do not keep backups of your data.

6. Permissions

Iter requires the following permissions to function:

  • Location (Always): Necessary for background tracking and manual anchors.
  • Background Refresh: Necessary for maintaining the journey log while the app is not in the foreground.
