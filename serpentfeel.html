HOW IT WORKS

01

HOLD THE DEVICE

Launch SerpentFeel and press WAKE THE SERPENT. The synthesis engine initialises — six layers spinning up in silence, waiting for motion.

02

MOVE TO SHAPE SOUND

Tilt forward and back to sweep the filter. Shake to push FM modulation. The accelerometer feeds directly into the synthesis core — your motion is the parameter.

03

ADD MIDI — OR DON'T

Pair a Bluetooth MIDI keyboard or connect via USB to route notes in. MIDI velocity controls dynamics; tilt still controls the texture. Motion and notes, woven together.

04

SEND TO YOUR DAW

Virtual MIDI output routes directly into Logic Pro, Ableton, or any audio workstation. Record the performance. Replay the motion.

05

FEEL THE SIGNAL

Haptic feedback synchronises with sound intensity — Thrum, Kinetic, or Heart mode. The device breathes with the sound in your hands.
