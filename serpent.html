Legal Documentation for SerpentFeel

1. PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

SerpentFeel ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection & Processing

SerpentFeel is a "Local-Only" application.

  • Sensor Data: The App uses your device’s Accelerometer, Gyroscope, and Magnetometer (via CoreMotion) to map motion to sound synthesis parameters.
  • Camera Access: The App uses the camera solely for AR-based spatial tracking (Spatial Mode) to calculate distance for audio modulation.
  • Haptics & Audio: The App utilizes the device's Haptic Engine and Audio Session for real-time feedback.

None of the data processed by these sensors is collected, stored, or transmitted to any external servers. All processing happens in real-time on your local device and is discarded immediately after use.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Children’s Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, the App is safe for users of all ages; however, we recommend parental supervision due to the intense nature of haptic feedback and high-frequency audio.
